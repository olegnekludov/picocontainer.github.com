Axis

PicoContainer Web Axis 1 support provides two implementations of Axis service providers that use the org.picocontainer.web.PicoServletContainerFilter to instantiate service classes and resolve their dependencies.

These are: org.picocontainer.web.axis.PicoMsgProvider and org.picocontainer.web.axis.PicoRPCProvider.
